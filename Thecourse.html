Code First: Girls runs free coding courses for young women who are either aged between 18-23 or are currently uni students/have graduated in the past 2 years.

Our courses usually run one evening each week for two hours, the beginners HTML/CSS is 6 weeks long and our advanced courses (Ruby or Python) are 8 weeks. There'll also be some socials and potentially a few guest speaker talks as well.

CodeFirst runns several courses in many cities in UK. The beginner courses run currently include:
